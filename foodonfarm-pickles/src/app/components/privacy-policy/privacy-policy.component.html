PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how foodonfarmpickles.com collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you visit or make a purchase from our store.

Collecting Personal Information

We collect information to operate our site, process your orders and provide customer support.

Device Information

  • Collected automatically through cookies, log files, web beacons and tags.
  • Includes browser type, IP address, time zone, and pages visited.
  • Used to load the site correctly and improve user experience.

Order Information

  • Collected when you place an order.
  • Includes name, billing and shipping address and payment information.
  • Used to fulfill orders, process payments and provide confirmations.

Customer Support Information

Information provided while contacting customer service is used to resolve issues and assist you.

Sharing Personal Information

We may share your data with:

  • Shopify (our store platform)
  • Payment processors
  • Delivery partners
  • Analytics and advertising partners

Behavioural Advertising

We may use your personal information for targeted ads or marketing.

Using Personal Information

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process and fulfill orders
  • Communicate with customers
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

Data Retention

We keep order information unless you request deletion where legally permissible.

Cookies

We use cookies for site functionality, analytics and advertising. Most browsers allow cookie control and removal.

Do Not Track

We do not alter our data collection practices in response to Do Not Track signals.

Changes

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version appears on this page.

Contact
