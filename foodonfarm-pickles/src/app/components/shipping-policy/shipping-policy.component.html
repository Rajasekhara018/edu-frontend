SHIPPING, RETURNS/REFUNDS AND CANCELLATION POLICY

Shipping & Returns/Refunds

Shipping

  • Standard shipping: Rs. 60. It takes 7-10 working days to prepare, dispatch and deliver the products.
  • Refunds are processed to customers for unsuccessful deliveries or for any issues related to items to their original payment method after coming to our notice.
  • We do not accept any kind of returns, but with regards to any kind of order related issues, we will be refunding the amounts within 48hrs of the issue coming to our notice.
  • Orders can be cancelled by the customer by contacting us through our WhatsApp customer care.
